How it works
Transparent by design.
Step 01

Paste your list

Copy the ingredient list from the back of any product — or type it in. INCI format or plain text both work.

Step 02

AI reads the formula

Inkredient identifies each ingredient and cross-references safety databases — EWG Skin Deep, EU CosIng, PubChem, FDA — flagging concerns with cited sources, not just colors.

Step 03

You decide

The score is a signal, not a verdict. Some flagged ingredients are fine for most people. Inkredient explains; you choose.

Disclaimer: Inkredient is an AI-powered educational tool, not a medical device. Ingredient safety assessments are based on general research and may not account for individual skin conditions, allergies, or sensitivities.
